About Me

I'm a Computer Science and Business Administration major at the University of Southern California, with a minor in Designing for Digital Experiences.

This fall, I began my Master's in Integrated Design, Business and Technology through USC's Progressive Degree Program.

Outside of my academics, I involve myself in diverse cultural and creative spaces that allow me to utilize my passions for technology and art to uplift underrepresented communities through creative expression.

Currently, I serve as the President of The Collective Identity Mentoring, a nonprofit organization dedicated to providing Black women and non-gender conforming students free mentorship, professional development, and personal growth.

Additionally, I serve as an Online Section Editor for the Daily Trojan, where I utilize Wordpress to create and update our webpages and develop Figma mockups for continous iterations of our website and application components.
